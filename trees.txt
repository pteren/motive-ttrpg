

Approach Tree - 

[Fool]
 +1 die when you try to outwit, manipulate, or decieve someone.
 success: npc gains anger when they discover the deception
 conseqence:
	[Betray]
	 +1 die when taking advantage of someone who trusts you
	 success: if the npc finds out you betrayed them, +1 BACKLASH
    [???]
	 until the deception is discovered, npc gains joy. if they still have this joy when the deception is discovered it becomes anger.

[Force]
 +1 die when you try to bend someone until they break.
 success: npc gains fear
 failure: npc gains anger instead
	[Threaten]
	 +1 die when you make a threat.
	 success: npc gains an additional fear
	[Hurt]
	 +1 die when you enact physical violence against someone
	 consequence: npc gains anger
	 
[Fraternize]
 +1 die when you try to build someone up, understand them, or connect with them.
 success: npc gains joy
 consequence: none
	[Flirt] 
	 +1 die if you pursue a romantic interest
	 success: you gain (trust/leverage??)
	 conseqence: npc has 2 Leverage against you.
	[Encourage]
	 +2 dice when you target a goal and support it, with their best interest in mind
	 consequence: if they get hurt, they may gain anger
	[Advise]
	 +1 die when you give someone genuine advice which you believe is in their best interest
	 success: they gain a motive. 
	 consequence: if it goes badly, they may gain anger regardless of your involvement.
	[Comfort]
	 +1 die when you put someone at ease
	 success: npc loses 1 fear.
	[Connect]
	 +1 die when you try to understand and relate to an NPC
		[Empathize]
		 +1 die if you can relate to the npc thru a shared experience you've had. doesn't have to be existing canon!
		['Insight']
		 Spend an extra hit on a Connect to reveal a Mask concealing the targeted motive.
		 if a Mask is revealed this way, the npc also learns something about you.
